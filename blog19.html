Blog 19: Mastering SEO

Since search engine crawlers struggle with JavaScript-heavy sites, we use specific techniques to ensure our Angular content is indexed.

  • Meta Tags: Using unique titles and descriptions for every page (like we did in this project!).
  • Clean URLs: Removing the '#' from the URL to make it look professional.
  • Sitemaps: Creating a list of all 20 blogs for Google to find easily.
