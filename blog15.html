Blog 15: Introduction to Routing

Routing allows you to navigate between different "views" in your app without the browser reloading the entire page.

The SPA Advantage:

Since only a portion of the page is updated, the website feels incredibly fast to the user. This is how modern apps like Gmail and Facebook work!

In Angular, we use the ngRoute module and the $routeProvider to map URLs to specific HTML templates.
