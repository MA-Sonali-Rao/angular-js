Blog 14: Services and Dependency Injection

In AngularJS, services are objects that are wired together using Dependency Injection (DI). You can use services to organize and share code across your app.

Why use Services?

  • They are "Singletons," meaning they only exist once in your app.
  • They are perfect for keeping data consistent between different pages.
  • They keep your controllers "thin" and organized.
